# Dear Bunny

## Overview

Dear Bunny is a digital publishing microbusiness focused on creating, managing, and scaling digital products through Amazon KDP.

The project serves two purposes:

1. Build a portfolio of digital products capable of generating passive income.
2. Serve as a professional case study demonstrating skills in Business Analysis, Product Management, Market Research, Data Analysis, and Project Management.

---

## Business Goal

Create a sustainable digital publishing business through Amazon KDP while documenting the entire process using professional project management practices.
