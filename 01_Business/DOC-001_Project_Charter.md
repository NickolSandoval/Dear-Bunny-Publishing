# 2. Business Background

Dear Bunny is a digital publishing microbusiness created to develop, publish, and manage digital products through Amazon KDP.

The project was born from two primary objectives:

1. Build a sustainable source of passive income through digital publishing.
2. Create a real-world professional project that demonstrates practical skills in Business Analysis, Product Management, Project Management, Market Research, Data Analysis, and Digital Product Development.

The business will begin with low-content products due to their lower complexity and reduced production costs, while gradually expanding toward more specialized and higher-value content.

---

# 3. Problem Statement

Many aspiring publishers enter Amazon KDP without a structured process for research, product development, documentation, or performance analysis.

This often leads to:

* Poor product-market fit.
* Inconsistent publishing decisions.
* Lack of business scalability.
* Limited ability to learn from previous launches.
* Difficulty building a professional portfolio.

---

# 4. Business Opportunity

Amazon KDP provides access to a global marketplace where digital publishing businesses can create scalable products with relatively low startup costs.

By combining structured business practices, market research, and continuous improvement, Dear Bunny can:

* Build a growing catalog of digital assets.
* Generate recurring revenue.
* Develop expertise in digital publishing.
* Produce measurable business results.
* Create a professional case study for future career opportunities.

---

# 5. Project Purpose

To establish Dear Bunny as a structured digital publishing business that develops and manages products through Amazon KDP while documenting the entire process as a professional portfolio project.

---

# 6. Project Objectives

## Primary Objective

# 12. Constraints

Current constraints include:

* Limited budget.
* Small team size.
* Limited publishing experience.
* Time availability outside other responsibilities.
* Dependence on Amazon KDP platform policies.

---

# 13. Assumptions

The project assumes that:

* Amazon KDP remains available as a publishing platform.
* Low-content products can be used to learn the publishing process.
* Consistent documentation will improve decision-making.
* Product quality and research will improve over time.
* The project team will maintain long-term commitment.
